ABOUT THE TEAM

The Trek Factory Downhill team was born on 1st January 2016 when Dan, Gee and Rachel Atherton signed to race for Trek Bicycles
The partnership marks the start of a new era for the Athertons, recognised around the world as the fastest family on two wheels. Between them they already hold 7 World Cha mpionships, 36 World Cups and 14 UK National titles.
The 2016 team will also include 19 year–old Taylor Vernon, a graduate of the Atherton Academy. This will be Taylor’s 4th season racing alongside Dan, Gee and Rachel.
Trek already have a successful road team and on the MTB side the Downhill team join a star-studded group of awesome riders across the cross-country team, Enduro team and C3 Project.
With Trek’s expert backing, their resources and commitment to developing the world’s fastest race bikes the Downhill riders are confident that there are many more victories to come
